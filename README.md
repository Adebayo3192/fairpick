# 🕌 FairPick — Halaqa Management System

A free, browser-based classroom management tool built specifically for Islamic halaqas and madrasahs. No app download, no subscription, no server — runs entirely in your browser.

🔗 **Live at:** [fairpick.netlify.app](https://fairpick.netlify.app)

---

## Features

### 🗓 Attendance Tracking
- Morning and evening sessions separately
- Saturday to Wednesday schedule
- Save records permanently per day
- Browse and edit past weeks
- Generate shareable daily report card image

### 📊 Daily Report Card
- Beautiful image with Gregorian + Hijri date (manually correctable)
- Morning and evening present/absent counts
- Share directly to WhatsApp staff group

### 🎱 Number Draw (Presentations)
- Students pick a physical number (e.g. 1–30)
- App draws randomly — whoever holds that number presents
- Fair rotation: no number repeats until all have been drawn
- Works for all students (boys, girls, kids)

### 🧹 Cleaning Duty Rotation
- Automatically selects from eligible boys (non-kids, present today)
- Fair rotation — yesterday's cleaner is skipped until everyone has had a turn
- Round-robin across sessions

### 👥 Student Management
- Add students with ID, name, category (Boy / Girl / Kid), and draw number
- Import students from CSV — no retyping on new devices
- Export student list as CSV backup

---

## Who is this for?

Any madrasah or halaqa with:
- Morning & evening sessions
- Saturday to Wednesday schedule
- Mixed student body (boys, girls, younger kids)
- Need for fair task and duty rotation
- WhatsApp-based staff communication

---

## How to use

Just open [fairpick.netlify.app](https://fairpick.netlify.app) on any browser — phone or desktop. No installation needed.

To self-host: download `index.html` and open it in any browser, or drag it onto [netlify.com/drop](https://netlify.com/drop).

---

## Data & Privacy

All data (students, attendance, history) is stored in your **browser's localStorage** — it never leaves your device. Export your student list as CSV regularly to keep a backup.
